Terms and Conditions

1. The Platform

Womshare is a technology platform that facilitates peer-to-peer carpooling. We do not provide transportation services. The platform connects independent female drivers with female passengers traveling on the same route.

2. Cost-Sharing Policy

In compliance with Indian laws, rides on Womshare are strictly for cost-sharing. Drivers must not charge more than the proportional fuel and toll costs for the trip. Womshare is for social commuting, not for profit-making commercial activities.

3. Gender Restriction

Womshare is strictly a women-only platform. Any attempt by a male user to create an account or use the service will result in an immediate permanent ban and potential legal action for misrepresentation.

4. Limitation of Liability

Womshare is not responsible for the behavior of users, vehicle condition, or any incidents occurring during a ride. Users agree to travel at their own risk and are encouraged to use the built-in safety features.
