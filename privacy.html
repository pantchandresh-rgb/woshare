Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

  • Identity: Name, Phone Number, and Government ID (for verification).
  • Location: Real-time GPS data during active rides for safety tracking.
  • Vehicle Data: Car model and vehicle number (for drivers).

Data Usage

We use your data only to facilitate rides and ensure safety. We never sell your personal information to third-party advertisers. Your phone number is only revealed to your confirmed co-travelers.
